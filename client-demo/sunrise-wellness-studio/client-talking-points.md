## #01 — Lead Capture & Instant Response

**Hook:** "Right now, when someone clicks your Instagram ad at 9PM, who replies to them at 9:01PM?"

**Cost of inaction:** A lead contacted within 5 minutes is **10x more likely** to convert than one contacted in an hour. If you're getting 80 leads/month and losing even 30% to slow follow-up, that's **24 lost trials** — at a 30% trial-to-paid rate and $79/mo over 14 months, that's **$7,900/month** of LTV walking out the door.

**What we built:** A 2-step lead capture funnel, a form that drops every lead into GHL within 60 seconds, and an automation that sends an SMS in under 5 minutes — even at 2AM — with a booking link.

**What changes for you:** Every lead, every channel, every time of day — auto-greeted, auto-tagged by source, and routed straight into the trial funnel without you doing anything.

## #02 — Trial-to-Paid Conversion

**Hook:** "How many of your free trials become paying members? Most studios are at 25–35%. We can get you to 50%+."

**Cost of inaction:** A trial that doesn't convert is the most expensive miss in the funnel — you've already spent the ad dollars to acquire them. At 60% non-conversion on 30 trials/month, that's **18 lost members each month**, or **$1,400+ in lost MRR every 30 days, compounding**.

**What we built:** A 7-day automated nurture across email and SMS that hits every concern a trial has — first class anxiety, "is this the right fit," the awkward "do I commit" moment — culminating in a personal-attention offer call on day 6 and a discounted-first-month CTA on day 7.

**What changes for you:** Trials no longer drift into silence. By day 7, every trial has either bought or formally said "not now" (which puts them in a longer-term nurture).

[Full pitch →](problems/02-trial-to-paid-conversion/README.md)

## #03 — Appointment No-Show Recovery

**Hook:** "What's your no-show rate on PT sessions right now? Industry average is 18%. Each no-show costs you a trainer hour you can't get back."

**Cost of inaction:** At 18% no-shows on 200 PT slots/month, that's **36 wasted hours**. At $85 a slot, that's **$3,060 in lost revenue every month** — plus trainer frustration, plus the member who feels too guilty to rebook.

**What we built:** A reminder sequence (48hr email, 24hr SMS, 2hr SMS), a no-show detection that fires the moment status changes, and a recovery sequence with a soft "we missed you, here's a one-click rebook link" SMS. No guilt, no friction.

**What changes for you:** No-show rate drops to 6–8% (typical), and the no-shows that *do* happen rebook themselves within 48 hours.

## #04 — New Member Onboarding

**Hook:** "Of every 10 new members you sign up, how many are still here at month 3? If you don't know, the answer is usually fewer than you'd guess."

**Cost of inaction:** The first 30 days predict 90-day retention. Members who don't attend in week 1 churn at **3x the rate**. Lose 4 of every 10 new members to early-stage drop-off, and at 20 new members/month, you've **silently lost $19,000+ in projected annual LTV** every single month.

**What we built:** A 30-day onboarding pipeline (Welcome → First Class → Two-Week Check-in → Month-1 Goal Review), with automated check-ins by email and SMS at days 1, 3, 7, 14, and 30, plus a triggered intervention if the member hasn't attended in 7 days.

**What changes for you:** Day-30 retention climbs from ~60% to 80%+. New members feel *seen* — not just billed.

## #05 — Retention & Churn Prevention

**Hook:** "When a member cancels, was the first warning sign their email — or did you already know two months ago that they'd stopped showing up?"

**Cost of inaction:** Silent churn is the worst kind because there's no chance to save it. Every churned Basic = $79/mo × ~14 months = **$1,100+ in lost LTV per loss**. A studio with 250 members losing 5% per month is losing **~$13,750/month in LTV** to preventable churn.

**What we built:** An engagement-scoring workflow that watches attendance, app activity, and message responses. Drops below threshold? Auto-tag "at-risk," fire a personalized win-them-back email from the owner, and offer a free PT session or class buddy invite to re-engage.

**What changes for you:** You catch at-risk members 4–8 weeks before they would have cancelled — and you save 30–40% of them.

[Full pitch →](problems/05-retention-and-churn-prevention/README.md)

## #06 — Upsell & Cross-Sell

**Hook:** "Your Basic members are paying $79. Some of them would happily pay $149 — but you've never asked. Same for PT add-ons and nutrition."

**Cost of inaction:** The average member's LTV **doubles** with even one add-on (PT package or nutrition). If 15% of your Basic members would upgrade and you're not asking, you're leaving **~$1,500/mo per 100 members** on the table.

**What we built:** Behavior-triggered upsell offers — Basic members who attend 12+ classes/month get a "you'd love Premium" email; Premium members hitting 4+ PT sessions/month get a "VIP saves you money" SMS; new members get a soft nutrition consult offer at day 21.

**What changes for you:** Upsells stop being awkward in-person asks and become automated, well-timed nudges that feel like service, not selling.

## #07 — Reviews & Reputation

**Hook:** "73% of people check Google reviews before they walk into a wellness studio. How many reviews did you add last month?"

**Cost of inaction:** Every month without new reviews, your Google ranking decays. Competitors with 200+ reviews outrank you. Studios with 4.7+ stars and 100+ reviews get **42% more cold leads from Google search** than studios with 4.3 and 25 reviews.

**What we built:** A post-class SMS asking for a review at the **peak moment** (the endorphin high after class), routed through a smart funnel: 5-star feeders go straight to Google; lower scores route to a private feedback form — so we capture the complaint privately, not publicly.

**What changes for you:** Steady inflow of authentic 5-star Google reviews, and bad experiences caught privately so you can fix them before they go public.

[Full pitch →](problems/07-reviews-and-reputation/README.md)

## #08 — Referral Engine

**Hook:** "Your happiest members already tell their friends about Sunrise. We just need to give them a reason to send the link instead of just mentioning it."

**Cost of inaction:** Referred members convert at **3–5x the rate** of cold leads and have the lowest acquisition cost (~$0). A studio of 200 members generating zero referrals is losing 8–15 new members/month they should be getting.

**What we built:** A tracked referral link unique to each member, a reward workflow (free PT session for the referrer, $20 off first month for the friend), automatic credit on signup, and a quarterly "top referrer" recognition.

**What changes for you:** Word-of-mouth becomes a measurable channel. You see who refers, who they refer, who converts — and you reward the behavior.

## #09 — Win-Back Lapsed Members

**Hook:** "Every cancelled member is a warm lead. They've already proven they'll pay you. Why are you treating them like strangers?"

**Cost of inaction:** Lapsed members convert at **roughly 2x the rate** of cold ad-driven leads, at zero CAC. A studio cancelling 12 members/month and never winning any back is losing **2–3 easy reactivations every month** — worth $2,400+ in annual MRR per quarter.

**What we built:** A 90-day post-cancellation sequence — gentle goodbye at day 1, "we miss you" check-in at day 30, comeback offer (waived enrollment fee + first month $39) at day 60, last-call special at day 90. Plus an immediate intervention sequence for *involuntary* cancellations (failed payment).

**What changes for you:** 15–25% of cancellations come back within 90 days — at no acquisition cost.

[Full pitch →](problems/09-win-back-lapsed-members/README.md)

## #10 — Owner Reporting & Visibility

**Hook:** "Right now, when I ask you about your MRR trajectory or your trial conversion rate, do you have a number — or do you have to go look?"

**Cost of inaction:** You can't fix what you don't measure. Owners running on gut **overspend on ads by 25–40%** (because they don't know which channel converts), miss churn warnings until they're irreversible, and burn time pulling numbers manually that should land in their inbox.

**What we built:** A custom GHL dashboard showing the seven numbers that matter (new leads, trials pending, trial-to-paid rate, active members, MRR, at-risk members, net new this month), plus a Monday-morning email digest that lands in the owner's inbox before the first coffee.

**What changes for you:** You make decisions on data, not feelings. You spot trends before they become problems. You walk into every business conversation knowing your numbers.
